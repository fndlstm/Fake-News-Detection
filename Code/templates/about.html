About the System

Users who are interested in the authenticity of the news they are reading, and want to stay up to date with the latest legitimate news, the Fake News Detection Software is an Internet-based web application that will process the news article text entered into it, and use Deep Learning to help the user recognize the news entered to be fake or real. Unlike the current method of recognizing whether the news being read by the user is real or not, where users would depend on more traditional methods of recognition, or simply believing what they read online to be true, our product will help users eliminate the tedious task of further research related to the news they read, wasting precious time and putting in more effort than necessary into something that can be done for them through our Fake News Detection System.
